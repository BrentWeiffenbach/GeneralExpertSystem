INTERNAL OPERATIONS TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE
=================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
This guide covers multi-department operational troubleshooting procedures across HR, Finance, IT, and Facilities.

CHAPTERS
--------
1. HR Troubleshooting:
   - Payroll errors
   - Benefits login failures
   - Missing employee records

2. Finance Troubleshooting:
   - Expense portal errors
   - Receipt validation failures
   - Budget system timeouts

3. IT Troubleshooting:
   - Device failures
   - Permission issues
   - Software installation problems

4. Facilities Troubleshooting:
   - Badge access errors
   - Meeting room booking failures
   - Safety escalation workflow
